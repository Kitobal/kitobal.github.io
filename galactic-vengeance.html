Galactic Vengeance


Controls

WASD / Arrow Keys to move
Space Bar to Shoot

About

A simple top down Space Shooter I made a while back when learning to make 2D games in Unity.
This game is infinite, the waves spawn forever. There's no winning, only getting high scores.
The meteors are unkillable so, dodge them!

Story(?)

A rogue pilot seeks vengeance against the Empire that betrayed him.

GALACTIC VENGEANCE

Controls

WASD / Arrow Keys to move
Space Bar to Shoot

Story(?)

A rogue pilot seeks vengeance against the Empire that betrayed him.

About

A simple top down Space Shooter.
There is no winning in this game, only getting high scores.
The waves spawn forever. No power ups.
The meteors are unkillable so, dodge them!
